### Phase 1 Gap Resolution

The critical gap identified in Phase 1 verification (\_currentProcess never populated due to missing onProcessStarted callback wiring through CleaningService) has been **resolved**:
- `ICleaningService.CleanPluginAsync` now accepts `Action<Process>? onProcessStarted` parameter
- `CleaningService.CleanPluginAsync` forwards the callback to `ProcessExecutionService.ExecuteAsync`
- `CleaningOrchestrator.StartCleaningAsync` passes a lambda that sets `_currentProcess = proc`

This was verified by grep at lines 379-383 (CleaningOrchestrator), line 47 (CleaningService), line 93 (CleaningService forwarding).
